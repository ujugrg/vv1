Common issues and solutions for watching movies on Netflix.

Why is my Netflix movie quality poor?

+

Poor quality can be due to slow internet speed, device limitations, or platform settings. Try checking your internet speed, updating your device, or adjusting quality settings.

+

Look for the download icon (downward arrow) next to content. Tap it to download for offline viewing. Note that not all content is available for download.

+
