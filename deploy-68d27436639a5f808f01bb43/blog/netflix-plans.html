Netflix Plans Overview: What You Need to Know

Netflix offers three distinct plans in India, each designed to cater to different viewing preferences and budgets. Understanding these Netflix plans is crucial for making an informed decision about your streaming subscription.

In 2024, Netflix plans in India have been structured to provide maximum flexibility to users. Whether you're a solo viewer or part of a family, there's a Netflix plan that fits your needs and budget. Let's explore each Netflix plan in detail.

Netflix plans in India start from ₹199/month for Basic and go up to ₹649/month for Premium. Each Netflix plan offers different features, streaming quality, and device limits.
