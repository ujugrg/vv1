Shipping and Delivery Policy

At ValueVibe, we provide digital products and services. Please read our shipping and delivery policy carefully before making a purchase.

1. Digital Delivery

  • All our products are delivered digitally via email, Telegram, or through your account on our website.
  • There is no physical shipping involved for any of our products.

2. Delivery Time

  • Most digital products are delivered instantly after payment confirmation.
  • In rare cases, delivery may take up to 1 hour due to technical or verification reasons.
  • If you do not receive your product within 1 hour, please contact us immediately at valuevibe01@gmail.com or on Telegram @ValueVibe1.

3. Order Confirmation

  • After successful payment, you will receive an order confirmation email with details of your purchase and delivery instructions.
  • Please ensure your email address and contact details are correct at the time of purchase.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our shipping or delivery policy, please contact us at valuevibe01@gmail.com or on Telegram @ValueVibe1.
